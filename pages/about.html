About

My name is AKPET, Kohly M. Obaji, a mathematics statistian by field of study, a business-man and consultant by love and an agriculturist (more like an agricultural enthusiast than an agriculturist) by passion, currently working with Serinya International Services | Sedek Doors and Cabinets®.

Most importantly about me, though born a sinner, I'm a Christian redeemed by the blood of the lamb to be brought into perfection be the trying of my faith that patience may have her perfect work in me.

I'm Cross Riverian, (born and raised), with a simple and primal interest for sharing the word of God as according to understanding to as many as I can reach, that all may know the mercies, loving-kindness and goodness of the Lord.

Though born into a Christian family and brought up according to biblical teachings, I'd erred in following the principles that were thought me (for reason which at the time seemed justified), have stumbled, fallen and like the prodigal son had come to the point of realisation of how lost I was, stumbling in the dark like a blindman, like a flag on a pole blown about by the wind till its brought to rags.

But in all things I bless God for His mercies; for by His blood, He brought me out of condemnation into victory and called me His own. For this, the idea behind the "Maran 'athâ Project" is proclaiming the Lordship of the risen Christ in all the earth and to all generations
