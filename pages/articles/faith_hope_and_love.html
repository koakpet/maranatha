Faith, Hope and Love

And now abideth faith, hope, charity, these three; but the greatest of these is charity.
1 Corinthians 13:13 KJV

In understanding the letter of encouragement sent to the church at Corinth by Paul, three things are of note, that three things shall always remain, come what may, three things will last forever, FAITH, HOPE and LOVE (charity).

FAITH; according to the Merrian-Webster dictionary, is a strong belief or trust in someone, something or an entity. In the Christian walk, this is an esssential tool (and if i would say) only legal tender for interacting with the Godhead.

Quickly looking through scriptures, faith as defined in Hebrews 11, is knowing with an assurance though not exactly tangible to physical interactions, that what we hope for will be.

Now faith is the substance of things hoped for, the evidence of things not seen. But without faith it is impossible to please Him: for he that cometh to God must believe that He is, and that He is a rewarder of them that diligently seek Him
Hebrews 11:1 and 6 KJV
